Sister Tossing

Sister Tossing should be an Olympic event. Various forms of the game are very strenuous and competitive. It contains a degree of competitiveness lacking in most other Olympic events. And a recent poll of likely Olympic spectators revealed that 49% of the spectators prefer watching Sister Tossing to any other event, including tackle table tennis. In light of the previous reasons, it should be clear that Sister Tossing would make a great Olympic event.
