All About Me

I used to look like Jon Syverson's brother,
but now I look more mature.
I used to play with my neighbor,
but now I lie on my bed and do nothing.
I used to hate the taste of baked beans,
but now I hate it even more.
I used to be afraid of the basement,
but now I'm afraid of a 747 falling on my toes.
I used to believe thunder was angels bowling,
but not any more.
I used to not be able to cross the street,
but now I can go lots of places on my bike.
I used to get in trouble for fighting with my sister,
and up to now, that hasn't changed.
