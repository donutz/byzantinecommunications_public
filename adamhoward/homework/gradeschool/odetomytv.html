Ode to My TV

Oh my dear TV
Where would I be without thee?
What would I do?
What would I see?

I could be doing my homework,
Or brushing my hair
But only with you, my dear TV,
Can I sit and do nothing but stare

Without you, I could watch no
Star Trek: The Next Generation,
or Garfield and Friends:
only you can bridge the gap between the two ends.

I am so glad I have you,
my special TV,
I need you,
And you need me.
