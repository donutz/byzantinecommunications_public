A Book of Cinquains

heat
fiery, hot
steaming, burning, blazing
boiling like soup
warmth

cat
evasive, elusive
stalking, growling, sneaking
obscure as the shadows
feline

food
tasty, delicious
simmering, frying, baking
sweet as money
nourishment

Matthew B.
talkative, funny
singing, blabbering, gabbing
noisy as a trumpeting elephant
loudmouth

cars
noisy, polluting
crusing, turning, driving
quick as a cheetah
automobiles
