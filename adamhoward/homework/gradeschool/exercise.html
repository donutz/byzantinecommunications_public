Exercise Program

For our exercise program, you start out by warming up with a few stretches, such as back stretch, toe touch, butterfly, and arm circles.

After your stretching, you do 10 sit-ups, then rest 30 seconds, then 10 more. After that, take your pulse rate and rest for 45 seconds.

After that you do some stairclimbing for 3 minutes. The best place to do this is by going up a down escalator. After that take your pulse (take your pulse after every exercise).

Next (after resting 45 seconds) do 30 jumping jacks, rest for 15 seconds, then do 15 more. Rest one minute.

Do 10 push-ups next, rest for 35 seconds, then do 12 more. Rest for 1 minute.

Hop on your bike or exercise bike next, you're going to bicycle for 1 mile. After that, rest for 2 minutes.

Then you bench press a quarter of your weight, for 3 times. Rest 3 minutes.

For the next exercise you do as many pull-ups as you can. Then you rest for 45 seconds.

After that you jog in place for 1 minute and rest 30 seconds.

Next you hand on the pull-up bar with your chin above it for as long as you can. Then you rest for 30 seconds.

After that you lift dumbbells of 8-10 lbs 5 times per arm, rest for 20 seconds, then repeat.

After that, see other sheet.
