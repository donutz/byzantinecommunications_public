Headquartered in central Orange County, California, and with offices in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, Byzantine Communications has a long and rich history. Starting in the early 1990's as independent movie studio Byzantine Productions, Byzantine Communications has since expanded greatly. In addition to remaining faithful to its movie studio roots, Byzantine Communications can now be found on the cutting edge of computer and communications technology.
